Foundational Skills Training.
This refers to core learning modules that provide the essential knowledge and abilities needed for a particular role or area of expertise. These modules act as the building blocks for further development.
Track & Certify Progress.
This feature allows learners to monitor their learning journey. They can track their completion of modules, assess their understanding through quizzes or assignments, and potentially earn certificates upon successful completion of the program.
Limited Email Support Only.
This signifies that the learning platform offers email as the primary method for customer support. Learners can pose questions or seek clarification through email, but options like live chat or phone support might be unavailable.
Mobile-Friendly Learning.
This ensures the learning program is optimized for access on mobile devices like smartphones and tablets. Learners can access the modules, track progress, and potentially complete assessments while on-the-go, offering greater flexibility for busy schedules.
Self-Paced Learning.
This allows learners to progress through the training materials at their own speed. They can complete modules, revisit sections for review, and adjust their learning schedule based on their availability and needs.
